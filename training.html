Training and Empoverment

To empower is to make you confident and raise your self-esteem in carrying out various activities.

People who have fear in performing some activities and demanding for their rights need to be empowered. An empowerment training course is, therefore, a program undertaken so as to instill confidence and self-esteem into the learners. These courses are mainly suitable for employees in a company, institution or business so as to boost their productivity.

SNK-Consult Empowerment training is the practice of sharing information and rewards with employees by the management as a way of improving services and performance of a firm. Our Empowerment involves building the performance capacity of a team to increase competence in their organizational tasks.

  • Effective meetings.
  • Assertive horizontal and vertical communication.
  • Management of work teams.

We offer courses for companies aimed at different capacities and abilities adapted to the service provided, in different organizational areas, as for managers, as intermediate managers and the different levels of the organization, adapting to your demand and what best responds to what its needed.
